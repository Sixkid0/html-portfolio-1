About Me

Welcome to my Fashion Designer portfolio! I am Abdul-Azeez Fuhad Adegboyega a dedicated and visionary fashion designer with a passion for creating captivating and trend-setting Male dresses e.g Native dress, Vintage, Jalamia, Hoodie, Jorgers, co-operate dresses.

From the young age I have been drawn to the world of fashion inspired by its ability to express individuality and transform the way people feel. I pursued my formal education in fashion design, where i earned my technical skills and developed a keen eye for detail.

I have focused on creating designs that celebrate the human form, embracing its diversity and empowering individuals to enbrace their personal style. I am passionate about inclusive fashion and strive to ensure that my design cater to a wide range of body types and sizes.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I look forward to connecting with fellow creatives and fashion enthusiasts who share my love for all things stylish and innovative.
